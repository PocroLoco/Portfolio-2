Case #1 Recosia

Social Media App

During my time at Recosia, a friends-only privacy-focused social media platform, I played a significant role in building and enhancing the app. One of my main contributions was working on the website design, ensuring a clean and user-friendly interface. I also contributed to the development of the web application, implementing features and functionalities that would enhance the user experience.

Recosia aims to prioritize user privacy and foster genuine connections among friends. By focusing on a closed network of trusted individuals, the platform provides a safe and secure environment for users to interact and share content without concerns about data exploitation or privacy breaches.

I am proud to have been a part of the team that helped shape Recosia and bring its vision to life. With all the hard work and dedication put into its development, I am excited to announce that Recosia is scheduled to launch in the later half of 2023. Stay tuned for an innovative and privacy-conscious social media experience that puts your connections first.
